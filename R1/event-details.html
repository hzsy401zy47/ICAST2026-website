Welcome to Hong Kong!

Being a Special Administrative Region of China, Hong Kong is described as the Pearl of the Oriental which has a fusion of eastern and western style. It is famous for its iconic skyline, glorious harbor and enchanting and lush nature. In October, the weather in Hong Kong is usually pleasant and refreshing. The average temperature is between 24 °C and 29 °C.

As a world-famous shopping paradise and culinary capital, Hong Kong offers a huge variety of shopping and dining options to choose from. There are glitzy malls, chic side-street boutiques and a mix of markets. And you can enjoy delicious cuisines from Cantonese to Korean, Japanese or French.

Hong Kong has one of the world's highly developed and safest transport systems. Half of the world's population take less than five flying hours from their regions to Hong Kong. There are various ways to get around Hong Kong: rail, bus, minibus, tram, taxi or ferry. It also has train to Mainland China and ferry to Macau.
