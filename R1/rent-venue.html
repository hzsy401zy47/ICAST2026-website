Conference Venue

HKUST

The conference will be held at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), located in Clear Water Bay, Kowloon, Hong Kong. HKUST is renowned for its cutting-edge research facilities and beautiful campus, providing an ideal environment for academic exchange and networking.

Lo Kwee-Seong Building

Most of the conference sessions will be held in the Lo Kwee-Seong Building, which features multiple lecture halls and numerous classrooms. All venues are outfitted with modern amenities to facilitate dynamic presentations and discussions.

Transpotation

From the Airport

By MTR

1.Take Airport Express to “Tsing Yi Station”

2.Transfer to Tung Chung Line to Lai King Station and than tansfer to Tsuen Wan Line to Mong Kok Station

3.Transfer to Kwun Tong Line and get off at Choi Hung Station from Exit C1

4.Take the minibus No.11 to HKUST

By Bus

1.Take Cityflyer A28 and get off at Station "Kei Shun Special School;New Clear Water Bay Road"

2.Transfer to Bus 91M to HKUST

From the Railway Station

1.Take Tuen Ma Line to Diamond Hill Station and then transfer to Kwun Tong Line and get off at Choi Hung Station from Exit C1

2.Take the minibus No.11 to HKUST

By taxi

Taxis are usually hailed along the street, you may wait for taxis either a taxi queue at a hotel or a taxi stand. All taxis are metered and the taxi fare information is listed on the inside of the taxi. You may ask for a machine-printed receipt from drivers.
